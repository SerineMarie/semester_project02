admission

The entrace is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activitiy packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum

accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessiblitity ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display sings for the visually impaired.

food and drinks

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.
